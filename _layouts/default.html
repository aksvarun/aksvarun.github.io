Akshayvarun Subramanya

I am a third year PhD student at the Dept. of CSEE of UMBC, where I work on Machine learning and Computer vision. My advisor is Prof.Hamed Pirsiavash.

I spent the summer of 2019 as part of ATG group at Dolby Laboratories working on generative modeling for speech signals with Cong Zhou and Vivek Kumar.

I previously worked at Indian Institute of Science, where I was a research assistant for Prof.Venkatesh Babu at the Video Analytics Lab. I was also fortunate to work with Prof.Jelena Kovacevic and Siheng Chen as a Research Intern in the summer of 2015. I completed my undergraduate studies from ECE department of PES Institute of Technology (now PES University).
